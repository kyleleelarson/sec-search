Search over thirty-three thousand annual reports submitted by public companies to the SEC. Our data set spans the last 20 years for almost all S&P 500 and Russell 2000 companies. Enter a phrase to perform full-text search on the Item 1. Business and Item 1A. Risk Factors sections of the 10-K annual report.

This is best used as a research tool to discover market trends and to find companies by business profile or risk exposure. If you already know which company you are searching for, or for more advanced search options, it may be better to use the SEC's in-house EDGAR search functionality.
